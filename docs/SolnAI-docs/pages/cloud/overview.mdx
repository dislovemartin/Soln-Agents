# Private managed SolnAI

SolnAI cloud is the easiest way to trial and scale SolnAI for your business or personal use.

The Mintplex Labs team offers **isolated** and **private** instances of SolnAI that you can use to try out or scale up SolnAI for your business or personal use.

Each instance is hosted on an isolated AWS instance that is automatically updated and managed by the Mintplex Labs core team. Your data and resources are not shared with any other customers who are using our managed service.
